The party limped back to Ottahofn after the rift storm, got patched up by Kjarval's lieutenant Brynja in exchange for signing on for another mission, and picked up a new member — Neko, a hulking Necramechanica with a fixation on "kitty" (Ashen). After surviving an alley ambush (goblin boss killed, troll paid off with 5 copper), the party spent the evening at The Rotting Boot Inn where the Mothkin barkeep Julian Davros revealed himself as one of Lilith's spies and dropped major intel on the Rewritten, their leader Fionrir Starsundr, and the Order of the Ashen Rose. Morning brought Void Taint saves — Vergrad cleared, Yorika's whispers remain. The session ended at the docks with two leads: another storm mission or hunting a sea creature called "Shark Guts" for a 15gp bounty.

Session Recap

Previously…

The party stumbled back into Ottahofn after their first rift storm mission — battered, two members unconscious, and two carrying fresh Void Taint whispers in their heads. Not the triumphant return anyone hoped for, but hey — the shards got charged.

Back at the Barracks

The party met Brynja — a towering Valcarrion Cleric and Kjarval's right hand. She's stern, clinical, and immediately assessed the group with a mix of scepticism and grudging interest (she wrote "problematic" in her book while looking at them). She healed the downed members with Cure Wounds but made it clear this wasn't charity — Kjarval wants more shards, and the party is now signed up for another storm mission. Extra shards beyond quota earn 15sp each, which helps chip away at Vergrad's 36gp debt.

Neko arrived. A hulking Necramechanica lumbered down the path after the group, fixated on "kitty" (Ashen). Tim's first session. Neko got assigned to the party — another set of hands for the next mission.

A shadowy figure was spotted watching the party leave the barracks. It deliberately let itself be seen, then phased straight through a wall and vanished. Nobody knows who or what it was.

Alley Ambush

On the way through town, a goblin boss and his crew jumped the party in a narrow alley. Highlights: Neko cleaved the goblin boss in his first ever combat, Elenor critted with Eldritch Blast but then fell 30 feet and went down again (death saves and everything), and the real threat turned out to be Galthor — a troll who lumbered around the corner as backup. The party talked Galthor down with 4 copper + 1 apology copper. Turns out the goblin had persuaded the troll to shake down passers-by, and Galthor's alley has a fixed toll price. Loot: a scimitar (kept to sell) and a short bow + quiver (Yorika claimed them).

The Rotting Boot Inn

The party's first real social hub in Ottahofn. The Mothkin barkeep, Julian Davros, turned out to be one of Lilith's spies — and an ally of sorts to Lucien and Elenor. Over the course of the evening he dropped some serious intel:

  • The Rewritten serve someone called Fionrir Starsundr, who has the power to "rewrite" her followers — wiping their memories, binding them to service, marked by silver threads stitched below their eyes. The temporal energy from the rift storms gets shipped to Moonguard for this purpose.
  • Lilith has a small network of "familiars" (mortal agents) in Ottahofn: Julian himself, a leatherworker named Penelope Kresh (has access to the Rewritten), and Koal Dockhand at the docks (enslaved, not willing).
  • There's a member of the Order of the Ashen Rose hiding at the church in town. Kjarval gave them amnesty. Julian warned the party to stay well clear — the Order are dangerous and fanatical.

Other inn moments: Lucien performed for the crowd (rolled a 25 — the inn loved it), Elenor fed on an unconscious drugged person Julian provided (vampiric sustenance, apparently routine), and the party noticed Eirny Vefstari — an Aetherborn warlock whose form constantly shifts in and out of solidity. She has the Rewritten silver thread marks and something keeping her in Ottahofn. Mysterious.

Elenor dropped some Necramechanica knowledge — about a thousand exist, created before the Orrery, extremely rare. The souls inside them have "one of the strongest wills known" and an unfulfilled purpose they need to see through. Elenor's mother Severina apparently used to commission one to help with the produce back home.

Vergrad's lore moment. His race are called Thornwrought — guardians bonded to Phoernikstrees (mother trees). When a Phoernikseed grows into a Phoernikstree and bears its first fruit, the fruit sprouts a Thornwrought guardian. Vergrad is his mother tree's "first fruit." He's over 320 years old.

Morning After

Void Taint saves. Vergrad passed — his mind cleared, taint gone. Yorika failed — the whispers remain.

The party discussed their options over breakfast. Vergrad's debt keeps them anchored to Ottahofn, and the rift storm missions are the most reliable way to pay it off. Running was discussed and dismissed — they'd get a bounty placed and have no money for transport anyway. Julian was asked to scrounge supplies for the next mission.

The Docks

The party visited Koal Dockhand — a glowing green Eidolon dock hand, one of Lilith's enslaved familiars. He can't leave (they won't let him on boats anymore because he keeps trying to escape). Cole offered a lead: a sea creature called "Shark Guts" that can be lured from the ice shelves and sells for 15 gold. He pointed the party toward Imar Marrowhands — wears a bicorn hat, most of the flesh missing off his face — who's supposedly going out to hunt it.

Blood Pact

Ashen approached Neko and proposed a blood pact — family. Ashen cut his arm, Neko cut tubes releasing hydraulic fluid, they clasped hands. "We are brothers. Family means we will always stand together. Bitter end." As they spoke, Ashen had a flash of memory — golden light, grasping hands with another person, a feeling that he's said these words before. Then it was gone.

Brett noted that Neko's hydraulic fluid is "really bad for you." Consequences TBD.
